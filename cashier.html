Job Title:

Cashier

Job Description:

Cashiers are an entry level position at Organic Foods that are responsible for assisting customers through payment and acquisiton of products purchased from our store. Cashiers must be able to converse with customers and use the register and scanner to carry out transactions.

Qualifications and Requirements:

We can train our cashiers to be proficient in the related technology, but expect employees to be positive, punctual, and experienced in some math classes or similar fields.

Hours and Payment:

Our shift schedule is available upon hiring, at $12 an hour. Please include in your application if you have any conflicting commitments or other things we must consider when discussing shift schedules.
