Job Title:

Lifeguard at Auburn Municipal Pool

Job Description:

Lifeguards need to supervise swimming activities at Auburn Municipal Pool and ensure that all rules and guidelines are followed. They will save swimmers in danger of drowning or in distress, and administer first aid or CPR should the need arise. Lastly, they will teach or assist in teaching classes in the fundamentals of swimming whenever needed.

Qualifications and Requirements:

  • Swimming proficiency in all kinds of water conditions
  • Knowledge of methods for providing on-site emergency medical aid
  • Certified Red Cross Lifeguard Training Certificate, or equivalent
  • CPR Certification, or equivalent

    • Hours and Payment:

    8:00 am to 4:00 pm, Tuesdays and Thursdays, May 25 - August 12
    Payment is $14 an hour
