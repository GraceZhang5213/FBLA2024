    • Job Title:

    Waiter


    Job Description:

    Waiters are expected to greet and seat guests, take and deliver orders, and ensure customer satisfaction. Entry level employees may also be trained to be cashiers, dishwashers, or cooks. We would like to maintain a positive environment and wish for employees that can remain positive and cordial to customers. No experience is needed.

    Qualifications:

  • Must be at least 16 years of age
  • No criminal record
  • Must be punctual

    • Benefits and Pay:

    We pay $3 an hour and will reimburse employees for pay not earned in order to meet minimum wage in the case of lack of tips
