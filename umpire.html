Job Title:

Little League Umpire

Job Description:

Every year at the Auburn Rec Center, we hold a youth baseball tournament. We have more teams this year than we have had in the past, and would like some responsible teenagers to help referee the games. You will be expected to shadow an adult umpire for some time and learn the game before being allowed to referee on your own. No experience is needed, but we will expect you to have some knowledge of the game of baseball and for you to be willing to learn. Games will run from March through May, every Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday, and there will two to four games every day

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Have at least some knowledge of baseball rules and be willing to learn
  • Be patient with the young kids, and be prepared to handle irate parents
  • Show up to work on time everyday
  • Be at least 14 years of age
